# ExamSprint - Product Reveal Video Guide

Welcome! We are excited to have you on board to create the **Product Reveal Video** for **ExamSprint**, a next-generation Student Resource Sharing App. 

To help you craft a highly polished, minimalistic, and "Apple-esque" product reveal video, this document outlines the core theme, visual identity, and user flows of the application. The goal is to reflect the app's premium, modern, and fluid engineering in the video.

---

## 1. Core Visual Identity & Theme

ExamSprint’s design language is built on **Glassmorphism**, **Minimalism**, and **Fluid Motion**. It feels native, premium, and distraction-free.

### Colors & Lighting
*   **Vibe:** Think cinematic dark mode or pristine light mode, similar to Apple's WWDC showcases. The app heavily uses high-contrast interfaces.
*   **Hero Accent Color:** A vibrant **Electric Purple** (`#7C3AED` to `#9F67FF`). This should be the primary color for glowing elements, highlights, and CTA buttons in the video.
*   **Secondary Gradients:** We use Emerald Green (`#10B981`) for positive actions (like Joining a Class) and smooth gradients to bring life to buttons.
*   **Backgrounds:** 
    *   *Dark Mode:* Deep space/midnight blue (`#0B0B14` to `#13132A`). 
    *   *Light Mode:* Clean, frosty white/grey (`#F7F7FC` to `#FFFFFF`).

### Typography
*   **Headings / Display Text:** **Space Grotesk**. This font is geometric, futuristic, and makes the app feel like a modern AI tool. Use this for bold text overlays in the video.
*   **Body Text:** **Inter**. Highly legible and perfectly clean.

### The "Glass" Aesthetic
*   The UI heavily features frosted glass overlays (blur effects) with subtle translucent borders. 
*   **Video Tip:** Ensure that UI layers in the video have realistic background blurs (like iOS UI blur) to simulate the app's native visual depth. Drop shadows are soft and expansive (e.g., black with 8% opacity and 20px blur, or colored glow shadows beneath active elements).

---

## 2. Animation & Physics (The "Feel")

The application is programmed with premium interactive physics. Your video animations should mimic these behaviors:
*   **Bouncy & Responsive:** Icons and buttons scale down slightly when pressed and bounce back up organically. 
*   **Glow Pulses:** The floating action button (FAB) has an infinite, soft breathing/pulsing glow.
*   **Staggered Entrances:** When a menu opens (like the "Quick Actions" bottom sheet), the elements inside slide up and fade in sequentially (staggered animation).
*   **Pacing:** Start scenes with smooth, slow camera pans and use snappy (but not overly aggressive) easings (like `EaseOutCubic` or `EaseOutBack`) when UI elements appear.

---

## 3. Key Hero Flows to Showcase

To tell the story of ExamSprint, focus the video journey around these core interactions:

### Scene A: The Reveal (The Main Shell)
*   **The Shot:** A glamorous, shallow-depth-of-field shot of the device screen turning on, revealing the **Main Dashboard (Home)**.
*   **Focus:** The custom **Floating Navigation Bar**. It hovers slightly above the bottom of the screen, completely blurred (frosted glass), casting a soft shadow on the background.
*   **Action:** Show the user tapping different tabs (Home -> Classes -> AI -> Profile). The selected icons gently bounce and gain a glowing dot indicator beneath them.

### Scene B: The "Magic" Action (Quick Actions)
*   **The Shot:** Close up on the **Center '+' Button** located in the middle of the navigation bar.
*   **Action:** The button pulses. As it is tapped, it physically rotates and scales down slightly.
*   **The Reveal:** A frosted glass "Quick Actions" menu glides up from the bottom. The options — `"Create Class"` (Purple gradient icon) and `"Join Class"` (Emerald gradient icon) — stagger in beautifully one after the other. 

### Scene C: The Capabilities (Modules)
*   Show quick glimpses of the main modules:
    1.  **Home / Resource Hub:** Finding notes, PDFs, and tagged resources (Tags like 'Notes', 'PYQ', 'Slides' have their own pill-shaped colors).
    2.  **Study Groups (Classes):** Joining a cohort.
    3.  **AI Playground:** ExamSprint utilizes Gemini AI (as per the app's architecture). Showcase an intelligent search or summary generation with a subtle glowing aura.

---

## 4. Video Editing Guidelines for the Editor

1.  **Keep it Minimal:** Avoid noisy backgrounds. Use pure black (`#000000`), deep grey, or pure white studio environments to make the UI pop.
2.  **Device Mockups:** Use sleek, bezel-less device frames (like the latest iPhone Pro or an abstract glass slab). 
3.  **Camera Work:** Use slow zooms, dynamic lighting that glints off the "glass" UI elements, and macro shots of the typography (Space Grotesk) and gradient icons.
4.  **Sync to Audio:** Use a clean, atmospheric, and punchy electronic/ambient track. Sync the snappy UI animations (like the FAB tap or the Quick Action stagger) to the subtle beats or snaps in the music.
5.  **Text Callouts:** When displaying text overlays in the video explaining the features, use the **Space Grotesk** font with negative letter spacing (tight tracking) for a premium, Apple-like keynote aesthetic.
